About Virtual lab

In our virtual lab, we provide you with

  • Scientific experiments in the shape of VR 3d animation.
  • Encourage students  applying and understanding  difficult experiments.
  • protection from harmful experiments.
  • Save time, money and effort.
  • Build a staple community for all researchers in different fields
  • Encouraging people to enter the world of metaverse and interact with it.

We have made and developed a virtual lab in virtual reality because virtual reality technology and metameres are developing rapidly and the future is for it. In our laboratory, there are many chemical elements and the great diversity of these elements even contains many trace elements and chemical tools And devices that cannot be found in ordinary laboratories easily. One of the advantages is to reduce the risk to which the researcher is exposed. The researcher may be a child, so we will be able to teach children in an easy and simple way and make them test the results of chemical reactions themselves and see the result in the virtual world even without a small percentage of the risk. Thus, we may also have reduced the cost that is spent on practical experiments. I think that this program will become popular in chemical laboratories, pharmaceutical companies, institutes and schools
